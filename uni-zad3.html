Important Concepts

Cell Theory
All living organism are composed of cells, and all cell come from pre-exising cells.
Photosynthesis
The process by which green plants and some other organisms use sunlight to synthesize foods with the help of chlorophyll.
Natural Selection
The proscess through which species adapt to their environment as individuals with favorable traits survive and reproduce.
